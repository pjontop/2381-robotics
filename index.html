2381 Robotics is a club run at Colonel By Secondary School in Ottawa with multiple competitive teams working on robots for VEX EDR Robotics Competitions. The VEX Robotics Competition is a robotics competition for elementary through university students, where competitors are given a yearly challenge for which they must design, build, program, and drive a robot to complete the challenge as best as they can.

Click here to visit their website

The 2019-2020 VEX Robotics Competition Tower Takeover is played on a 12’x12’ square field. Two alliances, one “red” and one “blue”, composed of two teams each, compete in matches consisting of a fifteen second autonomous period, followed by a one minute and forty-five second driver controlled period. The object of the game is to attain a higher score than the opposing alliance by placing cubes in towers, or scoring cubes in goals.
